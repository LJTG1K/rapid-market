# Your First Haul Guide: How to Order from China via RAPID & Sugargoo

If you're new to the world of hauls—ordering affordable items directly from China—it might feel overwhelming at first. But it's actually way simpler than you think. This guide will walk you through every single step, so you know exactly what's happening from the moment you hit "pay" to when the package lands at your door.

---

## What Is a Haul?

A haul is just a shipment of items you've ordered from Chinese suppliers, bundled together and sent to you. RAPID makes this easy by connecting you directly to Sugargoo, a trusted middleman who handles sourcing, quality control, and shipping for you. No stress, no mystery.

---

## Step 1: Find Items Using RAPID Links

Start by browsing RAPID. We've curated product listings with direct links to items you can order. When you find something you like, click the link—it'll take you straight to the item in Sugargoo.

**No item on RAPID yet but you know what you want?** Use the **Free Agent** button in Sugargoo. Just describe what you're looking for, and Sugargoo's team will find it for you.

**Pro tip:** Save items to your cart as you browse. No rush—your cart stays there until you're ready to order.

---

## Step 2: Add to Cart & Checkout

Once you've found all your items, it's time to checkout:

1. Review your items and quantities
2. Add a **coupon code** if you have one (Sugargoo often has them)
3. Select your items for checkout
4. Review your order one more time
5. Choose your payment method (Apple Pay, credit card, etc.)
6. Enter your payment password
7. **Hit "Pay Now"**

That's it. Your order is live.

---

## Step 3: Sourcing Begins (A Few Hours)

Once you've paid, Sugargoo springs into action. They start sourcing—hunting down all your items from suppliers across China. This usually happens in just a few hours.

You'll see updates in your account as items are found and confirmed. It's actually pretty cool to watch.

**What's happening:** Sugargoo's team is making sure every item exists, checking availability, and confirming pricing. If something's out of stock, they'll let you know and offer alternatives.

---

## Step 4: Items Arrive at Packing Center (7 Days)

After sourcing is done, your items travel to Sugargoo's packing center. This takes about 7 days. Don't worry—this is actually a good thing.

**At the packing center, they do a quality check:**
- They inspect every item for damage
- They verify you got what you ordered
- They make sure everything is in sellable condition
- If anything's wrong, they handle it before it ships to you

This is your protection. You won't open a mystery box of broken stuff because Sugargoo's already made sure it's good.

**Pro tip:** You can request packaging upgrades (bubble wrap, extra boxes, etc.) at this stage if you want extra protection.

---

## Step 5: Choose Shipping & Ship Out

Once everything passes QC, it's time to ship. Here's where you choose how your parcel gets to you:

**GO EMS** is the most popular option. It's reliable, tracked the whole way, arriving based on the estimated time of the shipping you selected.

**Note:** Item Cost and Shipping Cost are two separate charges. You'll pay for your items first, then select and pay for your shipping method.

**Tracking arrives 3-5 hours after payment**, so you'll know your parcel is on the move.

**What happens next:**
- Your items get packaged and handed off to the shipping carrier
- You get a tracking number (if you don't already have one)
- The parcel travels from China to your country
- It clears customs (usually seamless)
- It's delivered to your address

---

## Step 6: Track Your Parcel & Wait

From the moment you get that tracking number, you can follow your parcel every step of the way. Shipping times vary by region, but expect 10-20 days.

Use your tracking number to check:
- When it left China
- When it hit your country
- When it's out for delivery
- When it's arrived

Most people check their tracking obsessively. It's part of the fun.

## Common Questions

**Q: What if something's damaged?**
A: Sugargoo's QC catches most issues before shipping. If something does arrive damaged, they handle claims with the supplier.

**Q: Can I combine multiple orders into one shipment?**
A: Yes. You can place multiple orders and Sugargoo will consolidate them at the packing center to save on shipping.

**Q: What if I want to add something after I've paid?**
A: You can place another order anytime. Just decide if you want to ship it separately or wait to consolidate.

**Q: Do I need to pay customs or anything extra?**
A: Sugargoo handles the customs declaration for you. Sometimes customs fees apply depending on value and location, but it's rare for small hauls.

**Q: How do I know my items are authentic?**
A: Sugargoo sources directly from verified suppliers. The quality check at the packing center is your safety net.
